# Delete your account and data — Money Manager Pro

You can delete your Money Manager Pro account and all associated data in two
ways. Choose whichever works for you.

## Option 1 — From inside the app (fastest)

1. Open Money Manager Pro on your phone.
2. Tap the **Profile** tab at the bottom.
3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap **Delete account**.
4. Confirm the deletion.

This permanently removes:

- Your account credentials (email, password).
- Your synced cloud data — transactions, accounts, categories, loans,
  investments, goals, and backups.
- Your locally stored data on the device you are using.

The operation is irreversible and completes within seconds.

## Option 2 — If you no longer have the app installed

If you've uninstalled Money Manager Pro and you still want your account and
its cloud data permanently erased, email us:

- **Email:** [shashi.k@abitindia.com](mailto:shashi.k@abitindia.com)
- **Subject:** Delete my account
- **Send from:** the email address you used to sign up

We'll verify the request and complete the deletion **within 30 days**, and
confirm by reply. Backups and any other cloud copies are removed as part of
the same process.

## What is deleted, and what (if anything) is retained

**Deleted within 30 days of your request:**

- Account credentials (email, password)
- Profile fields (display name, phone number, avatar)
- All cloud-stored financial data (transactions, accounts, categories, loans,
  investments, goals, recurring rules)
- All cloud backups and snapshots
- Your device registrations

**Retained only where required by applicable law:**

- Subscription / billing records held by Google Play for tax-compliance
  purposes (we do not store these — Google does, on its own retention
  schedule).
